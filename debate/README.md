# Debate Statements Board

A collection of debate topics and statements for English language learning.

## Topics Included

1. **Technology** - "Smartphones have made loneliness worse, not better."
2. **History** - "Germany has dealt with its past better than almost any country — and most nations never do."
3. **Education** - "Most of what schools teach is completely useless in real life."
4. **Artificial Intelligence** - "AI will make doctors unnecessary within 30 years."
5. **Culture** - "Watching a great film is just as intellectually valuable as reading a book."
6. **Reading & Literature** - "People who don't read books are at a serious disadvantage in life — whether they know it or not."
7. **Privacy** - "People who say 'I have nothing to hide' don't understand what privacy actually is."

## How to Use

Open `debate_statements_board.html` in a web browser to interact with the debate statements board. Click on any card to select it.

## Features

- Interactive card selection
- Categorized by topic
- Clean, responsive design
