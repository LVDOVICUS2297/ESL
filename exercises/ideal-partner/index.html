Grammar reference — useful constructions
___ is + adjective
"She is warm, witty, and emotionally mature."
___ has + noun phrase
"He has a very charismatic personality."
___ tends to be + adjective
"She tends to be laid-back and open-minded."
___ is the kind of person who...
"He's the kind of person who makes everyone feel welcome."
One thing I love about ___ is that...
"One thing I love about her is that she's so genuine."
What's important to me is that...
"What's important to me is that they're emotionally secure."
I'd like someone who is / has...
"I'd like someone who is adventurous and well-travelled."
___ doesn't have to be... but...
"He doesn't have to be wealthy, but he should be ambitious."
